Privacy Policy

Warranty Pro

This Privacy Policy explains how Warranty Pro accesses, stores, uses, and shares information when you use the Warranty Pro mobile application. Warranty Pro is a warranty, service, and AMC record management app designed to help users store product details, attachments, reminders, and related contact information.

Effective date: April 6, 2026 Contact: sha.group23@gmail.com Android package: com.pat.warrantypro

Local-first app

Warranty records, dates, supplier details, and attachments are primarily stored on your device, not on a developer-managed cloud service.

Ads

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google and its partners may process device and ad-related information as described in their own policies.

No account required

The current version of the app does not require you to create an account to use the main features.

1. Information We Access or Process

Depending on how you use the app, Warranty Pro may access or process the following categories of information:

  • Warranty and service information you enter, such as product name, brand, category, coverage type, purchase date, expiry date, supplier details, serial numbers, and service center contact details.
  • Attachments you choose to add, including invoice images, warranty card images, PDFs, or other supporting files.
  • Reminder settings and app preferences, such as region, date format, title color, app accent, reminder timing, and backup preferences.
  • Backup files you choose to create, import, or share.
  • Device and advertising information processed by Google AdMob and related Google services when ads are requested or displayed.

2. How We Use Information

Warranty Pro uses information to provide the app’s features, including:

  • Saving and organizing warranty, service, and AMC records.
  • Showing warranty details and attachments within the app.
  • Creating reminders about upcoming expiries.
  • Generating PDF summaries and backup files when you request them.
  • Opening your device’s camera, file picker, phone, email, share sheet, or related system tools only when you choose to use those features.
  • Displaying banner and interstitial ads through Google AdMob.

3. Storage and Local Processing

Warranty Pro is designed as a local-first app. Warranty records and preferences are stored locally on your device through local app storage and local database technology. Attachments are stored on the device or referenced from files you selected.

We do not currently provide a developer-operated online account system or a developer-managed cloud sync service for normal app use.

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. As a result, Google and its partners may collect or process certain data such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location signals, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and similar advertising-related information, subject to Google’s own policies and settings.

Warranty Pro may also use standard platform features or third-party components for:

  • Opening your email app when you choose to contact support.
  • Submitting app ratings or reviews through platform-provided review flows.

5. Permissions

The app may request access to device features only to support features you choose to use:

  • Camera: to capture invoice or warranty images.
  • Files / Photos / Media: to select attachments and open saved files.
  • Notifications: to send expiry reminders.
  • Internet / Network access: required for ad delivery and certain third-party services.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared only in the following limited cases:

  • With Google AdMob and related Google ad services when ads are displayed.
  • With the apps, services, or recipients you choose when you share a PDF, backup file, email, or other exported content.
  • With your device’s operating system and installed apps when you explicitly choose actions such as call, email, file open, or share.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Warranty records and preferences remain on your device until you edit, delete, reset, uninstall, or otherwise remove them.
  • Attachments remain on the device or referenced location unless you delete them through the app or from your device storage.
  • Backup files remain where they were created or shared until you delete them.
  • If you uninstall the app, local app data stored in the app sandbox is generally removed by the operating system, but files you exported or intentionally stored elsewhere may remain until you delete them manually.

8. Security

We use reasonable efforts to keep locally stored data protected within the app’s normal platform environment. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting access to your device and any exported files or backups.

9. Children’s Privacy

Warranty Pro is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly design the app to collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

10. International Users

If you use the app in a jurisdiction with privacy rights or consent requirements, including regions covered by advertising or consent rules, you may also be subject to additional notices or choices provided by platform or advertising partners.
