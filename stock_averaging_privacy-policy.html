Privacy Policy for Stock Averaging App

Effective date: March 28, 2026

Stock Averaging App ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use the mobile application.

Information We Collect

The app allows you to enter stock quantities, prices, and trade values in order to calculate average price and position value. This information is processed locally on your device for app functionality.

We do not require account creation or direct sign-in to use the app.

Advertising Data

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob and its partners may collect certain information automatically, such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • App interaction and diagnostics data

This information may be used to provide, improve, and measure ads. Data collected by Google is handled according to Google's own policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

How Google uses information from apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Provide stock averaging calculations inside the app
  • Display ads through Google AdMob
  • Maintain app stability and performance

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not directly share the values you enter in the calculator with our own servers because the app does not include a backend service for storing user calculation data.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may process limited technical and advertising-related data as described above.

Data Storage

The calculation inputs you enter are used locally within the app session. Based on the current app implementation, we do not provide user accounts or our own cloud storage for this data.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable steps to limit data handling within the app. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can stop using the app at any time by uninstalling it.

For information about how Google manages advertising data, please review Google's privacy controls and policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the effective date on this page.
